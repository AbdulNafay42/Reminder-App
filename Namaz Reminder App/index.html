What is namaz?

“Salat is also known as namaz (Persian: نماز), are prayers performed by Muslims. Facing the qibla, the direction of the Kaaba with respect to those praying, many to most Muslims pray first standing and later kneeling or sitting on the ground, reciting prescribed prayers and phrases from the Quran as they bow and prostrate themselves in between. The daily obligatory prayers collectively form the second of the five pillars in Islam,
